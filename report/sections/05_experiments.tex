\subsection{Detection Training (Module 1)}
YOLOv8m was fine-tuned from COCO-pretrained weights on the SKU-110K train split. Key configuration:

\begin{table}[t]
\centering
\caption{YOLOv8m / SKU-110K training configuration.}
\label{tab:train-config}
\begin{tabular}{ll}
\toprule
Setting & Value \\
\midrule
Model            & YOLOv8m (COCO-initialized) \\
Image size       & $1280 \times 1280$ \\
Batch            & auto ($\sim$60\% VRAM) \\
LR schedule      & cosine \\
Epoch budget     & 50 (upper bound) \\
Wall-clock cap   & 9.5\,h (Ultralytics \texttt{time}) \\
GPU              & 1$\times$ NVIDIA L4 (24\,GB) \\
Host             & GCP \texttt{g2-standard-8} \\
\bottomrule
\end{tabular}
\end{table}

Training ran on an on-demand GCP L4 VM that synced all artifacts to Cloud Storage and then
self-deleted, bounding cost. Evaluation uses the SKU-110K validation split, reporting mAP@0.5,
mAP@0.5:0.95, precision, and recall.
